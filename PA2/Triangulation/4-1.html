DiscreteMath PA2 - 4

Prerequisite

Do not enter two identical points.
This is used to distinguish polygon with hole.
Do not enter a point that is located on the line.
ex) (00)(10,10)(20,20) -> (00,0)(20,20)

Input format

The most recommended type of input is the pairing of the x and y coordinates in parentheses.
(x1, y1) (x2, y2), (x3, y3) ……
But it doesn't matter much.
You just need to separate the numbers with parentheses or commas.

Instruction

Please enter the coordinates of the simple polygon you want to draw like input format above.
When the polygon button is pressed, a simple polygon is drawn.
When the draw button is pressed, triangulation results is drawn.
If the polygon is not a simple polygon, the message ‘it is not a simple polygon’ is printed.
Clear button clears the canvas, and reset button initializes the input box.
